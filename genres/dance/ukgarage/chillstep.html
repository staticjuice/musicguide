CHILLSTEP


Chillstep set out to be the complete opposite of Brostep. Instead of being heavy and percussive, the music was calm and full of beautiful harmonies and melodies. Unlike Liquid Dubstep however, Chillstep did not set out to be upbeat and club-friendly, but completely chilled out to be designed for relaxing listening environments – music that you could even fall asleep to. As a result of this, the quintessential "wub-wub" sound that came to be associated with Dubstep is almost never found in Chillstep tracks, instead being replaced by acoustic instruments and/or lovely atmospheric textures. In recent years, Chillstep (alongside Liquid Dubstep) has also fallen out of favour due to the rise of Future Bass, but also more genre-defying chilled-out electronic music. There aren't really any notable musicians who pioneered this style, but the YouTube channel known as MrSuicideSheep helped popularize it and bring a lot of otherwise-unknown Chillstep artists into the spotlight. One could even say that the whole concept of Chillstep itself was out to prove to the world that Dubstep can be a beautiful genre and that it's not all about aggression.
