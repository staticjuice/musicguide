ALTERNATIVE R&B

2011

Alternative R&B is a term used to describe a stylistic alternative to Contemporary R&B that came to prominence in the early to mid 2010s. This unconventional style has been described as an exchange between EDM, Hip Hop and R&B's commercial avant-garde. Brandon Neasman of The Grio observes a "changing of the guard in R&B, from the smooth, cool heartthrobs to these vulnerable, off-kilter personalities" amid the prevalence of social media in society. A lot of the production is echo-laden and lofty, often using a lot of synthesisers and filtered drums – sonically giving a nod to Prince's vintage 80s sound.
