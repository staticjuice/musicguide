WONKY

2008

At the beginning of the 2010s, Glitch Hop merged with a number of genres into a much larger style that can be described as a sort of off-kilter and low-slung form of Hip Hop. Apart from Dubstep-influenced beats, the main characteristic of Wonky are its heavily distorted mid-frequency synthlines that seem to be broken by their unstable or "wonky" sound. The "wet and unstable" sound of Wonky is achieved by producing mid-range basses using pitch bending, LFOs on lowpassing and highpassing as well as phasing and delaying. The resonance parameters of the synth's LFOs are often high, giving the synth and bass unique "wonky" sounds. Wonky can be regarded as the "IDM" of Hip Hop – experimenting with unexpected sounds, interrupts and unconventional techniques. Many Wonky artists also reside at Warp Records, the principal IDM label. Wonky has some overlapping with Post-Dubstep and forms therefore another bridge between Hip Hop and UK Garage or Drum & Bass, next to EDM Trap. Lately these supergenres have become more intertwined leading to some interesting results.
