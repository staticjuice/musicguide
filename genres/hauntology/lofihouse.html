LOFI HOUSE

2016

Lofi House combines elements of House music with the warm, nostalgic qualities of lo-fi production. Emerging in the mid-2010s, it is characterised by its mellow beats, muted basslines, and the use of analogue synths and vinyl crackle to create a retro, almost vintage feel. The production often includes imperfections like tape hiss, background noise, and a slightly detuned sound, which contribute to its laid-back and intimate atmosphere. Artists in this scene often draw inspiration from 90s House music, blending it with modern lo-fi aesthetics to create tracks that are both danceable and relaxing. The genre is perfect for listeners seeking a chill, atmospheric vibe that still maintains the rhythmic drive of House music. Lofi House is also known for its use of soulful samples and emotive melodies, which evoke a sense of nostalgia and warmth. The visual aesthetics often align with the music, featuring vintage imagery and subdued, earthy tones. Overall, Lofi House offers a refreshing, down-to-earth take on electronic music, providing a serene escape through its soothing and nostalgic soundscapes.
