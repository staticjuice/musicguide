PSYCHOBILLY


Psychobilly is a weird and unusual fusion genre that never received much attention, although it has a recognizable sound and substantial subculture. In the late seventies, Punk artists were drawn towards Rockabilly. The mixture of these genres processed a remarkable outcome – creepy Rock & Roll with haunting echos, spooky sound effects and rough chainsaw riffs. It is downright crazy music, like the band had lost their brain and became zombified, hence the word Psychobilly. Vocals are also often sung in a stuttering manner, reminiscent of Rockabilly. Horror Punk is a genre defined by a large amount of nostalgia for fifties imagery, fashion and themes and especially horror films. Horror Punk shares a great deal of overlapping with Psychobilly as both genres like to indulge in a subculture of B-flicks and cult movies.
