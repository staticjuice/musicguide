NEO-PSYCHEDELIA

2007

Neo-Psychedelia is a modern revival of the Psychedelic Rock movement of the 60s, characterised by its dreamy soundscapes, experimental approaches, and vibrant aesthetics. This version blends the hallucinogenic sounds of the past with contemporary production techniques and Alternative influences. Key features of Neo-Psychedelia include lush, reverb-drenched guitars, swirling synthesizers, and intricate layers of sound that create a trippy, immersive listening experience. The music often incorporates elements of electronic music, giving it a modern twist. Lyrics explore themes of existential musings and altered states of consciousness, enhancing the genre's ethereal quality. Tame Impala has been pivotal in bringing Neo-Psychedelia to a mainstream audience. Their work showcases a seamless blend of retro psychedelia and modern Rock, using catchy melodies and rich textures. The band's success has inspired a wave of contemporary acts to explore similar sonic territories. This style remains influential, with many artists drawing inspiration from its expansive, mind-bending sound.
