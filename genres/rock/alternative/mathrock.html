MATH ROCK

1992

If there would be such a thing as "Intelligent Rock Music" or "IRM", it would probably be Math Rock. Pioneering artists can be traced back as early as 1988, but Math Rock takes its mature form after the emergence of Post Rock. Its name is derived from experimenting with asymmetrical complex time signatures that give a jagged and chaotic sound. These experiments are so technical and far-reaching, that "mathematical" is a proper way to describe them. Particularly the pronounced drum rhythms (with breaks and interrupts) are treated this way. Drums take lead over voice, which becomes (along with the lyrics) less important. Math Rock and Mathcore obviously have a common "mathematical" dimension, but they are very different genres. In 1993, the first traces of Mathcore appear. Because Mathcore evolved alongside Metalcore and eventually becomes a subgenre of it, the difference between Mathcore and Math Metal is minimal. Mathcore combines the speed and energy of Hardcore and Metal with the complexity and non-structure of Jazz and unconventional Classical Music.
