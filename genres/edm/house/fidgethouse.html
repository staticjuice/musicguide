Electro House has a tendency towards snappy, glitchy, and fidgety sounds. A tendency that gets stretched further by the lesser known Fidget House genre. It uses glitchy beats and Rave elements for synth stabs, while chopped vocals take their cue from House. Fidget House was very short-lived and rather obscure, starting as a small experiment (actually a joke), but it gained more popularity than expected. The genre's playful nature and innovative use of sound elements make it a favourite among those looking for an energetic and unconventional dance music experience. Though the peak of Fidget House was in the late 00s, its influence persists.
