TECH HOUSE

1994

Tech House is exactly what you would expect – a mix of House and Techno. The sound of House is remixed on top of a Minimal Techno beat in digital productions, originally in a clean and minimal production style that was associated with Techno. Tech House has no famous hits or anthems – it is a massive abundance of DJ-targeted records specifically made for mixing. This is because it's the safest Dance music any DJ can play as it never strays too far into one category or another. It's not too melodic, catchy or ecstatic to be straight House so it can be easily mixed and dissolved as a continuous beat in the background. Neither is it too rhythmical or minimal to be pure Techno. Tech House features more distortion (often on the kicks), shorter sounds, more pronounced snares, and less warm/more synth melodies than regular House. It is, however, more structured than Techno and often features vocal samples or more eclectic influences, and equally as much raw electronic sounds in their music. However, a rich Techno-like kick and bassline seems to be a consistency amongst Tech House music.
