FUNKY BREAKS

1996

Funky Breaks was a progression of Florida Breaks that's all about groove and rhythm. It takes the raw energy of breakbeats and infuses them with funky basslines, syncopated drums, and samples that give it a playful, upbeat vibe. The music is driven by a strong sense of rhythm, with tight drum loops that keep things moving while the basslines add a layer of bounce. Funky Breaks is less about aggressive, in-your-face beats and more about getting people to dance with style. The tracks often feature samples from classic Funk records, chopped and rearranged to create something fresh but still rooted in the sound. You'll hear horn stabs, guitar riffs, and vocal snippets all woven together over a steady breakbeat foundation.
