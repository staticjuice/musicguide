HARDSTYLE

1999

Hardstyle developed when a group of people tried to recreate the original, casual feeling of early Gabber but without any explicit reference towards the genre. Hardstyle is a massive genre, incorporating tons of songs, composers and multiple subgenres. Though still largely underground, Hardstyle has survived since its birth and still has a strong fanbase in many cities around the world. The probable reason for this popularity is a good combination of the appealing factors of EDM's heaviest genres – Gabber, Hard Trance and Hard NRG, which makes Hardstyle a flexible jack of all trades (as long as it's ultra-hard and loud). The "slower" tempo and abrasive sounds of Hard Trance, the focus on synth stabs and vocal samples of Hard NRG, and the typical distortion on kick drums or melodies of Gabber are the key ingredients of Hardstyle's spicy melting pot. After a few years, a very popular subgenre known as Jumpstyle arrives. Afterwards, Jumpstyle fused with Techno and becomes Tekstyle – a genre that is still in its infancy but quite promising.
