PSYCHEDELIC TRANCE

1995

Goa Trance and Psytrance, two technically different but very similar genres, started early in the province of Goa, India. Here, spiritual refugee hippies sought new forms of psychedelic music as Psychedelic Rock was no longer hip. When Trance was invented, it quickly became the preferred music of choice, lending itself perfectly to accompany hallucinogenic drug experiences. Almost every element of Psytrance is based on hallucinogenic drugs and trip experiences, particularly magic mushrooms, LSD and DMT. But Goa was not just a refuge – it was the destination of a long and adventurous hippie trail, starting in Ibiza, towards Marrakech, then the Middle East, to Kathmandu and then finally India. With all these influences, Psytrance rapidly transformed into the most complex, multi-layered dance music ever invented – a tsunami of psychedelic sound effects, intricate melodies, and hyperclean, punctuated kick beats. Psytrance often uses more simultaneous tracks than any other music genre – it is the perfect soundtrack for a rapid transcendental journey into the unconscious mind.
