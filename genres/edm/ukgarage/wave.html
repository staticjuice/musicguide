WAVE

2017

Wave, a subgenre of Trap, emerged in the mid-2010s and is distinguished by its atmospheric melodies and harmonies, melodic and heavy bass such as reese, modern trap drums, chopped vocal samples processed with reverb and delay, and arpeggiators. Visually, it incorporates computer-generated imagery and animation, and imagery from video games and cartoons. Wave conveys feelings and qualities of melancholy similarly to Witch House and Emo Rap, dreaminess, sci-fi akin to grime, femininity, and otherworldliness. Wave emphasises melodic and harmonic aspects in combination to drawing from styles such as Trance and Synthwave for interludes and drum beats. Melodies in Wave music tend to be haunting and reflective, evoking a sense of melancholy. Wave has carved out a unique niche within the broader Trap and Electronic music communities, praised for its ability to convey deep emotions through its lush soundscapes and evocative melodies. This is one of a few genres to evolve out of UK Garage recently that incorporates a cyberpunk aesthetic, as a renewed interest in Cyberpunk occurred.
