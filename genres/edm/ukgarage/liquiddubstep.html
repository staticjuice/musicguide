LIQUID DUBSTEP

2011

Since the rise of Dubstep's popularity largely coincided with the rise of Liquid Drum & Bass, some artists decided to forge a go-between of the two – Liquid Dubstep. This is essentially just Liquid Drum & Bass instrumentation and melody fused with the rhythms of Dubstep. The result is a much more accessible variation of Dubstep that is both upbeat and melodic, as opposed to the purely harsh sounds of Brostep that everyone was familiar with. However, the heavy bass sounds of Brostep are often still present, but this time they are secondary instruments, usually with intense pads and orchestration in the foreground instead. A large wave of diverse and emotional tracks have been released since, until it largely became overshadowed by the Future Bass sound and then later developments. The Liquid genres prove that sometimes Dance music isn't only about massive drops and aggression – it can also be all about feeling emotions. Despite this, the genre is still upbeat enough to be played in large clubs as it retains much of the same energy as its predecessor.
