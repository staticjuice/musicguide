SPEED GARAGE

1997

Speed Garage emerged in the mid-90s in the United Kingdom. It is characterised by its fusion of House music's soulful vibes with the heavier, more syncopated rhythms of Jungle and Drum & Bass. Speed Garage is known for its distinctive basslines, shuffling beats, and use of time-stretched vocals. It uses a "wobble" or "warped" bass sound, which provides a deep, throbbing foundation for the tracks.
